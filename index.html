About Us

Setting up and managing a start-up can be quite a challenge for an aspiring entrepreneur in this immensely competitive world. Most entrepreneurs must face up to limitations in investment capabilities, as well as quickly deal with the (often harsh) realities of a business vertical/market.

Hungry Wallet is an initiative, which helps growing IT businesses become sustainable and profitable. Hungry Wallet is there to support nascent businesses explore new territories and ventures. We can help entrepreneurs operate in a more cost-efficient way while providing them with valuable resources and expertise that are key to their success.

True to its name, Hungry Wallet is a resource-connecting and opportunity-mining platform only for those hungry entrepreneurs, who aspire to boosting revenue and grow as fast a rate as possible to feed their (hungry) wallet.

We Have a Right Solution!

We know the secrets of startups

For any entrepreneur, a start-up is a dream come true. For it to succeed, a fine balance of business strategy and wise cash flow needs to be stricken. Some start-ups lose steam at their early stages because they have not invested their capital wisely and have run out of cash, whilst others find it difficult to pool in efficient technical and non-technical resources. At Hungry Wallet, we educate, mentor and guide business newcomers to help you break even as early as feasible. We have built up wealth of experience over the years within the tech/IT arena and are equipped to helping you staff and resource your future at an affordable spend.
